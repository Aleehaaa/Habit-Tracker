Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Habit Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Habit Tracker, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username and password that you create during registration
  • Habit Data: The habits you track, completion status, and dates
  • Preferences: Your color choices, bedtime settings, and display preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our service:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features you use most
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain the Habit Tracker functionality
  • Personalization: To customize your experience based on your preferences
  • Progress Tracking: To calculate and display your habit completion statistics
  • App Improvement: To understand how users interact with our app and improve features
  • Security: To protect your account and maintain the security of our service

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We do not have access to your personal habit information
  • Your data is tied to your browser and device
  • Clearing browser data will delete your habit history

3.2 Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are handled securely (if authentication is implemented)
  • No sensitive data is transmitted to external servers
  • Regular security updates and maintenance

Important: Since data is stored locally, we recommend regularly backing up your browser data or exporting your habits if this feature becomes available.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and follow these principles:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties
  • No Advertising: We do not use your data for advertising purposes
  • No Analytics Tracking: We do not send your personal data to analytics services

4.1 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your habit data within the app at any time
  • Modification: You can edit or delete your habits whenever you choose
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data (if export feature is available)
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the service at any time

5.1 How to Delete Your Data

To completely remove your data:

  1. Log out of your account
  2. Clear your browser's localStorage
  3. Clear browser cookies and cache

6. Cookies and Tracking

Habit Tracker uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login and session management
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We do not use cookies from external services
  • No Tracking Pixels: We do not use tracking pixels or beacons

7. Children's Privacy

Habit Tracker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Data Retention

Your habit data is retained locally on your device indefinitely or until you:

  • Delete your habits manually
  • Clear your browser data
  • Uninstall the application
  • Request account deletion

10. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data remains in your jurisdiction.
