Empowering Millions to Build Better Habits

We believe that small, consistent actions lead to remarkable transformations. Our mission is to make habit-building simple, effective, and accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Habitify was born from a simple observation: most people struggle not because they lack motivation, but because they lack the right tools to track and maintain their habits.

In 2018, our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of building consistent routines while juggling busy professional and personal lives. Frustrated by complicated apps and overwhelming systems, they set out to create something different.

Today, Habitify serves over 2 million users worldwide, helping them transform their lives one habit at a time. Our app has been featured as App of the Day on the Apple App Store and has received recognition from productivity experts globally.
